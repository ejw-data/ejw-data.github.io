Promoting Organizational and Personal Excellence

Ever since my days as a college intern, technically called a co-operative education student (Co-op), I have believed that every organization and in many cases people should define their own corporate policy statement. My first true job was at Eastman Chemical and I knew their Corporate Responsibility and other policy documents very well. The short documents gave me a definitive source supporting my good stewardship. Some of my favorite statements are: Eastman Chemical Values, Eastman Chemical Corporate Responsibility, Diversity and Inclusion, and Responsible Care.

This webpage was created as a way of showcasing my past work experience and skill sets that I have developed over the years. My academic background is in engineering and education so I thought that I should at least express my motivations that may be overlooked in sections about my education, career, and outreach. To provide clarity of my own positions and values, I have included a brief outline below. My advocacy is mostly focused around the application of education and technology.



Mission Statement:

Envisioning what we should learn and how to learn effectively



Values:

  • Rigorous Yet Balanced Expectations
  • Skill-based Educational Content
  • Continuous 'Time-Averaged' Assessments
  • Pro-social Communication Skills
  • Strong Leadership and Ethical, Smart Management
  • Promotion of Diversity and Inclusion
Remember

Kindness, Honesty, Knowledgeable, and Being Resolute are the Qualities of a Thoughtful Leader
