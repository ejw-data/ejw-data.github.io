Messaging Me

Thank you for visiting my website. Please contact me by using the form on this page for any of the following reasons:

  • You found a mistake or inaccuracy
  • A page is not loading or is difficult to read
  • You would like to provide feedback about a posted opinion article
  • You would like to suggest an accessibility option for a disability
  • You would like to suggest content to be added to the website

Please only use my email address for the following:

  • You are interested in hiring me as a consultant
  • You are looking for collaborators on a research project
  • You are looking for members and board members for a non-profit

My email address is found in the upper right corner of the website.


Calling Me

This method is typically reliable but if you do not hear from me within 7 days then feel free to send me an email.
